Our Story

Born from a garage roaster and a dream.

We started roasting on a hand-cranked drum and serving friends out of a tiny alley window. Today, we’re still obsessed with the same three things: great beans, great water, and great people.

We rotate seasonal origins, publish brew guides, and donate 1% of profits to local literacy programs.
